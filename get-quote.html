FAQs

Is this the right time to do my home interiors? +


Yes, it is. From meetings at our Experience Centres to installing your home interiors at the site. So don't worry about kick-starting your dream project. Since the process of co-creating a design typically takes 4-6 weeks, you can get started with online consultation. Our designers can assist you online via video calls. You can also schedule an appointment and meet us at our Experience Centre.

Can I meet my interior designer? +


Yes, certainly. Keeping safety in mind, we have enabled 2 different ways of interactions:
1. Online consultation
2. Safe design meets at our Experience Centres.

Can I have a meeting at my home? +


We would recommend meetings at our Experience Centre visit. We are taking every step to make it a safe environment. However, you can always opt for online consultation and we will be happy to discuss your project in detail.

How can I select materials for design? +


While we can share images of the materials and finishes during the design phase online, you will have to visit our Experience Centre to finalise them.

How will my site be measured? +


You can share the floor plan of your home with our designers, that should help us to start your project. If in case you don't have the floor plan, we will assign our representative for a site visit.

What will be the timelines for my project completion? +


We will keep you updated on the timelines based on the discussion with the designers. We are doing everything we can to cover up on the lost time and to deliver your home interiors in the safest way possible.
